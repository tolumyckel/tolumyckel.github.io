Our Love Story

Boy meets Girl

I met this Damsel for the first time in December 2017 at a Christian conference in Ottawa. I was new to the city and only knew a few people(my hosts and some other folks). After the first session, I think she was actually the first unfamiliar face that greeted me on her way out. Following that, we saw again and never missed each other at the fellowship we both attended. We remained good friends and nothing more until November 12th, 2019, when the scale fell off my eyes….

Tolu

Girl meets Boy

What seemed like an ordinary introduction progressed slowly into an amazing friendship with Tolu. Slow because I had to move to Chalk River shortly after we met, which was a two hour drive from Ottawa. Depsite our reduced contact with each other, our friendship and conversations always picked up from wherever we left it.

Seun

The Opening of the Eyes

Here's the story of how my eyes got opened. I went into walmart on this faithful day of meeting the woman of my dreams, with no idea of what was awaiting me. As God did arrange it, I ran into her at the check out and saw her looking a little uneasy. She had lost the card she was going to use to pay for her groceries so I had to step in to help the damsel in distress. What I didn't know was that I was taking a leap into a new swirl of emotions for Seun. I caught a glimpse of her beauty and couldn't stop thinking about her. Naso she jazz me sooo...

Tolu

The Resistance

Fast foward to 2020, after I moved back to Ottawa, I noticed something had changed about the way Tolu related with me - the intentionality, mehn, it was undeniable. I read no meaning to his gestures and just always dismissed them as innocent. But as the calls increased, I started to get suspicious of his intentions and sought for ways to discourage any further gestures suggestive of a love interest. This was the moment things got pretty dramatic between us - Tolu trying to pursue me and I frustrating all his efforts to win my heart. I refused to cooperate and only delayed the budding of a now beautiful love affair. I soon realized it was the mercy of God toward me, that planted that persistent desire in his heart for me.

Seun

How it's going

We owe our gratitude to God for bringing this relationship to its current depth and strength. Neither of us would have it differently. So far so good, it's been a joy of a ride, finding pleasure in the existence of each other. We look forward to all the blessings that await us on this adventure of our marriage and covet the prayers of you our loved ones.

Tolu & Seun
